Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Skillipop

How do I create an account?

Click the "Register" button in the top navigation and fill out the form with your details. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within 5 minutes.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

Most of our courses are designed for beginners with no prior experience. Some advanced courses may recommend completing foundational courses first.
